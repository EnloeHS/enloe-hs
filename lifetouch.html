  • Enloe HS Class of 2016 Senior Portrait Update

      • Valued Partner,
      The Class of 2016 is ready for their Senior Portraits - and so are we!  I wanted to review some components of our program this year so you can help us ensure we're providing a once-in-a-lifetime portrait experience!

      • We will be photographing the Class of 2016 seniors at our studio in Raleigh over the summer.  Here is the studio address:
        • 5108-B Oak Park Road, Raleigh, NC 27612
      • We will be at your school in the fall for a make-up day scheduled in conjunction with the appropriate school contact (s).
      • Your incoming seniors will be mailed a brochure detailing all of our sitting options and portrait sessions.
      • This mailing will include a letter that highlights information about yearbook clothing requirements (if applicable) and yearbook photo deadlines.
      • They will have two options for scheduling their portrait session...they may:
        • Call our office:  919-377-0026
        • Go online at  prestigeportraits.com/raleigh
          • Our website will go "live" for scheduling on May 15th
      • This year, parents and students will be given an opportunity to opt-in to receive program information and reminders directly from us via text message if they send a text to a specific number...we will be sharing more detail on this exciting new development in the days ahead.
      • We will be providing you some announcement banners and posters to reinforce program awareness and to help you promote program participation!
      • We are sponsoring a contest extended to all yearbook advisors to reward schools that achieve targeted percentages of the total senior class photographed.
