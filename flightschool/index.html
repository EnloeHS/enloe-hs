Welcome, Enloe Class of 2019!

EAGLE FLIGHT SCHOOL

Hello class of 2019! We look forward to meeting you this summer at Enloe's freshman orientation, Flight School. Students, teachers, counselors, administrators, and parents are putting together information and planning exciting activities so that you can get a feel of the school before the first day. We look forward to welcoming you into the Enloe family.

What will I Do At FLIGHT School?

Students: You'll learn about Enloe's clubs and organizations. Counselors will lead sessions to teach you how to be a successful student. And to give you confidence for the first day, students will lead you on a tour. After seeing a slideshow and a senior fashion show, meeting counselors and other freshmen, and exploring campus with upperclassmen, you will get a chance to walk your schedule.

Parents: You'll hear about Enloe and all it has to offer. There will be a question and answer session for you to learn what you want to know. A representative from student services will offer you advice on how to help your high school student be successful and what to expect. You will also learn about booster clubs and be able to sign up for newsletters and as a volunteer.

***Parents and students will have an opportunity to make schedule changes at the end of Flight School if necessary.

(Important Tip: Arrive at camp dressed in comfortable clothing that's appropriate for teambuilding activities. A T-shirt, tennis shoes, and shorts that are acceptable under Wake Clounty's dress code are good choices.)
