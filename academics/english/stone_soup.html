Enloe Literary Organization

Stone Soup is a literary magazine published annually by the Enloe Literary Organization. The magazine only accepts submissions created by the Enloe student body. The club meets once every other Thursday from 2:30pm to 3:30pm in Room 2203. Club Poster

Submitting to the Magazine

Submitting work for consideration in the ELO Stone Soup magazine is a simple process. Work can be submitted through English teachers, through ELO'S online account at enloeelo@gmail.com, or through direct submission to editors or Ms. Chappell, the ELO faculty advisor. Work is accepted from the beginning of the year through the beginning of March. No special format is required for the submission itself; handwritten and typed alike are accepted, although digital copies are preferred. However, we do ask that submission forms accompany all submissions. The form allows the editors to communicate with contributors and also certifies the authenticity of the contributor's role in the creation of the work. Submission forms may be picked up from Ms. Chappell's room, 2203 West, and are also availible online. We are not in a position to accept work from faculty or the general community. We are always eager for submissions and look forward to future contributions from the Enloe student body next year.
