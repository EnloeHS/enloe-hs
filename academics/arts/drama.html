Click here to go to the Enloe Theatre website

Something for Everyone

Drama offers an exciting, comprehensive variety of opportunities for beginning through advanced levels of study and experience. Courses include work in creativity as well as performance, skills, and stagecraft. Acting and special interest classes provide students with performance opportunities in and outside of class. Drama students also explore other areas of the theatre such as history, play analysis, and technical theatre design and production. Co-curricular productions such as musicals, non-musicals, one-acts, original plays, and student-directed works, are produced each year.

Parent Opportunities

Parents are a vital part of Enloe Theater. They are needed to run concessions stand and ticket sale for each show. Also needed are chaperones for trips. Funds are raised throughout the year to support Enloe Theatre. Sometimes fundraising opportunities are targeted for special uses such as our scholarship fund or for personal funds for trips. New ideas from parents are always appreciated.

Parents are also welcome to attend any class. Please contact the teacher to let them know you want to attend. On the day of the visit, go to the main office and sign in. They will give you a visitor pass so that you can go to the classroom.
