Visual and Performing Arts: TV Broadcasting

Television Production

The television production curriculum is designed for all students who have an interest in video. If you are just curious about how a television program is produced, just take one semester - TV Productions 1. However, if you are interested in television as a career, you may take as many as four years of TV Production.

Our students have won a total of 6 Emmy nominations and 2 Emmys in the past 4 years. Graduates of Enloe's production program can go straight into entry level jobs in television or can attend college and major in related specialty areas such as journalism, drama, literature, or business. We have graduates working at Sony Pictures and in Industrial Light and Music in California, in television with CBS, ESPN, ABC, and local stations around the country. Some students have attended summer production programs at Northwestern University and UCLA.

The 'Loe Down

The Loe Down is a daily, live, informational program designed to give students the information they need to be fully involved in campus life. Airing during second period, students are given the opportunity to physically appear on The Loe Down and make school-wide announcements on a daily basis. This efficient, effective means of communication combines the need to make announcements and the talents of advanced tv production students.
