Visual and Performing Arts: Vocal Music

Vocal Music

Enloe has an outstanding reputation for its high quality vocal music program. The choirs have performed at Carnegie Hall, St. Patrick's Cathedral, and the Cathedral of St. John, the Divine, in New York City. In the summer of 1992, they did a major tour of Spain with an orchestra from Moscow, visiting the Summer Olympics in Barcelona and the World's fair in Seville.

Last spring the Chamber Choir won 1st place and the Most Outstanding Choir Overall at Disney World. In June, 1994, the combined choirs represented North Carolina at the Allied Music Salute for the 50th Anniversary Celebration of D-Day in Normandy, France. They also performed concerts in Paris and London.
