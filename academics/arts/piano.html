Visual and Performing Arts: Piano

Excel Offstage

In addition to the performing groups, there are classes offered for students interested in music in a non-performing setting. These classes range from appreciation and exploratory classes to the challenging music theory classes and a creative composition class.

Piano Lab

Enloe is the only high school in Wake County that offers piano. No prior experience is required, and students who have previously studied piano are grouped according to skill level. Classes are small which allows for one-to-one instruction. Piano Lab is offered both as a semester and year long course. Also students can elect to take Piano Lab all through their high school career.
