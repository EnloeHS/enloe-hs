Career and Tech Mission

Striving for Achievement

  • To go above and beyond.
  • To inspire students to find their passion.
  • To help students reach their full potential.
  • To give students real world experience.
  • To actively engage ALL students.
  • To care.

Excellence Through Example

Enloe strives to provide our students with real world experiences, as well as provide them with the tools to succeed in today's business and technological environment. We aim to guide our students toward excellence, through our example, through our expertise, and through our love for the learning environment.
