What is Computer Science?

If you are interested in learning how computers work and function, then Computer Science is the thing for you. If you've ever found yourself wondering, "How does games designed? Could I actually program software/games for a living?", "How do you create an app or game for my phone?, then you might be interested in Computer Science. Let Computer Science be your first step to becoming a cool software designer for the next great development in personal computing!

Enloe's Computer Science department offers a variety of curriculum opportunities to its students. Use the links to the right to learn more about the offerings, and to explore the possibilities of a Computer Science education.
