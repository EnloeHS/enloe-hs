The Enloe Arts Guild

 

Enriching Arts Programs at Enloe

 

Visual arts * dance * audio and television production* theatre * piano * chorus * orchestra

 

The Enloe Arts Guild is a non-profit corporation, created by parents to provide financial support for the Arts teachers and their students at Enloe High School.

Enloe gives each Arts department just a few dollars per student for supplies. For example, last year, the entire theatre department including all classes for three teachers had to make do with $660 to purchase necessary supplies for 250-300 students. This money goes toward everything from fixing soundboards to purchasing the scripts used in the classroom to purchasing current versions of computer programs, such as Photoshop, for teachers. While funding varies by department, every Enloe arts program struggles to find the funds to deliver a professional-quality program.

The Enloe Arts Guild is here to help. Your membership dues and contributions go towards funding items that improve the classroom experience for your students.
