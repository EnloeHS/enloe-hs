Enloe PTSA Meetings/Minutes

2013-2014 PTSA Meetings/Programs

Enloe PTSA welcomes you to our meetings. We usually meet at 7 pm in the Media Center, West Building, except where otherwise noted. PTSA Meetings begin with a brief business section, including updates from the Principal, PTSA President, Treasurer, and some Committee Chairs. Then we present interesting speakers and topics of relevance to you and your student. Meeting dates and topic descriptions are announced in the Enloe e-news.

Our fall Program schedule is below:

  • September 17 -- Enloe 101 and Home Base
  • October 15 -- Driver's Ed
  • November 19 -- College Admissions and Financial Planning
  • December -- no meeting

Our Spring 2014 program schedule is below (note different times and locations):

  • January -- no meeting
  • Friday February 7 -- Summer Opportunities Fair, 4-6 pm, East Gym Lobby; PTSA Meeting at 6 pm in Room 1804
  • Tuesday, March 11 -- Common Core Standards, 7 pm, Media Center
  • Tuesday, April 22 -- Drugs Uncovered, 6 pm, Auditorium
  • Wednesday, May 21 -- The First Year College Experience, 7 pm, Media Center

Check back for future program dates and topics. We welcome your ideas and interests. Please send any program suggestions
to the PTSA Programs Chairs, Younghee Overly, yoverly@gmail.com , and Maria Murray Riemann, murrayriemann@me.com .
