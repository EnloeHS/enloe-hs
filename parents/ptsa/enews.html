eNews: Weekly PTSA Email Newsletter

Each week through email, eNews works to be informative and helpful for Enloe parents by sharing news, listing upcoming events and providing many helpful website links. To receive eNews, send an email to enloehsenews-subscribe@yahoogroups.com . You will receive an email confirming your request to join; simply reply to this confirmation email. You will later receive another email welcoming you to the Enloe Parents Yahoo Group.
Please note the following:

  • eNews includes announcements from Enloe staff and PTSA leadership on important information for parents. Main topics include News, Upcoming Events, College Corner, and a listing of Web Links.
  • eNews also can be used to issue timely announcements during the week, as needed. Generally, eNews sends one, sometimes 2 emails per week.
  • Text is worded concisely to be efficient.
  • Only the group moderator can send messages to the group.
  • Any email address works; you are not required to have a Yahoo email.
  • Your email address will not be used for any other purpose.
  • You can unsubscribe at any time by sending an email to enloehsenews-unsubscribe@yahoogroups.com.

Should you ever have trouble with eNews, please feel free to email enloehsenews@yahoo.com for assistance. Also, we are always open to news to share and suggestions for improvement. Please just let us know!
