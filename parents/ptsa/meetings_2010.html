2009-2010 Enloe PTSA Meetings/Minutes

Scheduled meetings of the Enloe PTSA take place at 7:00pm in the Media Center, located in the West Building on campus, unless noted otherwise. Meeting dates for this year are as follows:

(Minutes are viewable as a PDF file by clicking on meeting date)

  • Sept. 1, 2009 Budget approval, meet the officers, membership and committee sign-up
  • Oct. 13, 2009 Program - “Enloe 101 for Parents”
  • Nov. 10, 2009 Parents Only Program - Drugs Uncovered by POE Center (6:30-8:30 pm in auditorium)
  • Jan 12, 2010 Program - College Financing and Summer Opportunities
  • Feb 9, 2010 Program - “Safety Awareness” presented by Highway Patrol
  • March 2, 2010 Programs - “Best Fit for College” and 2010-2011 Registration
  • April 6, 2010 Reflections Recognitions Event
  • May 11, 2010 “Celebrate Community" and Board Officer Elections
