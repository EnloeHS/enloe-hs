Enloe PTSA Meetings/Minutes

2014-2015 PTSA Meetings/Programs

Enloe PTSA welcomes you to our meetings. We usually meet at 7 pm in the Media Center, West Building, except where otherwise noted. PTSA Meetings begin with a brief business section, including updates from the Principal, PTSA President, Treasurer, and some Committee Chairs. Then we present interesting speakers and topics of relevance to you and your student. Meeting dates and topic descriptions are announced in the Enloe e-news.

  • First PTSA Meeting/Program:  September 16 at 7 pm in the Auditorium, following CDM Parent Information Session at 6 pm
     Election for Enloe PTSA Board of Directors for 2014-2015 will be held at the PTSA Meeting.    
  • Tuesday, October 21st at 7 p.m. in the Media Center.  Program:  Enloe 101
    Come out to hear from a panel that includes administrators, teachers and student council representatives to get a crash course on everything you need to know about Enloe.  Topics will include Driver's Ed and Homebase and many other topics of importance to new and returning Enloe families.
  • Tuesday, November 18 at 7 pm in the Media Center. Program: The Financial Impact of a Good College Fit
    Miles and Letha Costin, Regional Directors of the American College Planning Foundation, will join us to discuss the college application and financial aid process. Philomena Karol, Enloe's Dean of Students, will also be present to discuss college readiness and admissions information for NC public and private colleges and universities. 
  • Tuesday, January 27 at 7 pm in the Media Center.
    Program:     Meeting followed by Enloe's own version of the film Race to Nowhere. We will have a panel of Enloe seniors sharing their personal stories of achieving balance during their high school careers. Also, Ms. Ina Nyko, Enloe's school psychologist, will discuss anxiety, depression and stress. If you haven't had a chance to view the movie and are interested, it is now available for download at: racetonowhere.vhx.tv
  • Tuesday, February 17 at 7 pm in the Media Center.  
    Program:     Program will focus on opportunities for high schoolers over the summer.  There will be representatives from Interfaith Food Shuttle, Habitat for Humanity, Raleigh Aquatics, Raleigh Parks and Rec, City of Raleigh Summer Youth Employment and NC Virtual Public Schools to discuss options and answer questions.  Various organizations such as Youth Empowered Solutions, Shelton Challenge, Wake Tech, Wade Edwards Learning Lab, the YMCA and area hospitals will also have information at the meeting.  Students, come see how you can get involved in your community this summer!  
  • Tuesday, April 14 at 7 pm in the Media Center.
    Program: PTSA program on Safe Driving. Parents and students, please join us for our next PTSA  meeting for a teen safe driving presentation by Officer Vincent Espinoza from the Raleigh Police Department.  Officer Espinoza has extensive experience working with the Raleigh Youth Council and their successful DRIFT (Drive Responsible Initiative for Teens) program.  He will discuss what parents can do to help reduce at risk behaviors with teen drivers, distracted driving and conversation points that should be addressed prior to allowing our teenagers behind the wheel.  Please join us for this informative session! 
  • Tuesday, 19 May at 6:30 pm in the Media Center.
    Program: PTSA will hold its final general membership meeting of this year on Tuesday, 19 May 2015 at 6:30 pm in the Media Center. Parents are encouraged to attend as the slate of PTSA officers for the 2015-2016 school year will be presented for vote.

Check back for future program dates and topics. We welcome your ideas and interests. Please send any program suggestions to Susan Gowland, gowlandfamily@yahoo.com .
