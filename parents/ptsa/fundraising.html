Painless Fundraising

Enloe and the Enloe PTSA raise funds through partnership with businesses that donate a fraction of the cost of purchases to the school. The funds earned from shopping are sent directly to Enloe.

  • Please read the instructions below and SIGN-UP ï¿½ it is so easy!
  • Ask your friends and relatives ANYWHERE these stores are located to register and shop.
  • You must RE-link your grocery cards at the beginning of EVERY school year.
  • Make sure that Enloe is your designated school for your Target REDcard.

AmazonSmile

  1. Go to " smile.amazon.com "
  2. Sign in (if needed)
  3. Put Enloe in the Non-profit box

Harris Teeter VIC- Together in Education

Harris Teeter shoppers can link VIC cards online www.harristeeter.com/community/together_in_education/link_to_your_school.aspx

Or ask the cashier to link your card to Enloe at the checkout. A percentage of the purchase price of prescriptions and a variety of Harris Teeter brands are donated to the school. Our code is #1796

Kroger Plus Card for Education

Go to the customer service desk and ask to link to William G. Enloe High School. For more information, click here. Our school code is #90222.

Office Depot -- Five Percent Back to Schools Program

Ask to link to William G. Enloe High School. For more information, click here. Give cashier our school code # 70062185.

Target REDcard - Taking Charge of Education

Use your Target REDcard when shopping at Target. Target will donate 1% of your purchases to our school. If you already have a Target REDcard, call 1.800.316.6142 and designate Enloe High School. If you are applying for a new card, indicate Enloe High School on the application. Click here for more information. Our code is # 81114.

Corporate Giving

Some corporations (such as Glaxo, IBM, SAS) will match employee contributions or will donate funds to nonprofits for volunteer hours worked by their employees. Contact your company's Human Resources or Personnel offices to see if and in what way they might augment your contribution to Enloe.
