Clubs

Academic Clubs

Have a passion for biology? Interested in showcasing your hidden writing talents? Want to further your knowledge in computer science? Explore Enloe's Academic Clubs, the perfect way to expand the horizons of learning beyond the classroom. Meet peers with similar interests and seek out unique educational opportunities that most interest you. If knowledge is your passion, these are the clubs for you!

Interest Clubs

School isn't entirely academics - at Enloe, we want you to pursue your interests, too. From debate to cooking to Harry Potter, there are clubs for all kinds of hobbies. Whether you want to learn a new skill or meet students with similar talents, there is bound to be an Interest Club that fits your tastes.

Cultural Clubs

Looking to learn more about students different from yourself? Explore the rich history, beautiful language, exotic foods, and fascinating traditions of the students at this diverse school by participating in Cultural Clubs! Discover the world, one person at a time.

Performing Arts Clubs

Enloe Magnet High School has the largest and most comprehensive visual arts program in the Wake County Public School System. Visual Arts disciplines have myriad levels of study for students with interest in experiencing arts, and for students interested in pursuing a career in them.

Service Clubs

Home to the largest Key Club in North Carolina, Enloe is proud of its students' dedication to helping others. From Key Club to Beta Club to the National Honor Society, students work diligently to give back to the community. Make a difference and join a Service Club today!

Student Council

This site will provide you with information about Enloe High Schools' Student Council. The website includes: event dates and times, your class and student body representatives, event pictures, event signups, a comment box, and class council updates.
