Empowering Oppressed Genders

Description

Empowering oppressed genders is a club dedicated to spreading awareness about worldwide gender equality issues. We strive to provide a welcoming and understanding environment in which each member feels comfortable discussing gender inequality and we push for further education and growth within each member. In addition, we believe in reaching out to the community through different service projects as a way to tackle gender inequality.
