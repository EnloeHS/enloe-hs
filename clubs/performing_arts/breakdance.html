Breakdance Club

Description

This club is to promote Breakdancing and also other forms of dance in the hip-hop category such as locking, waving, and tutting. We will provide music, a dance floor, and lessons in breaking and other dance. This club is not just for current dancers, but also new dancers! We will hold tutorials and exercises for beginners and give tips and suggestions for people who need help.

Meetings

We will have meetings in Ms. Mackie's Dance Studio at Enloe once a month on either a Tuesday or Thursday.
