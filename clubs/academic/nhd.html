National History Day Club

Description

National History Day (NHD) is in its fourth year at Enloe. We are very proud to be the only high school in Wake County with a NHD program. NHD is a history competition in which you choose between five categories: Individual Paper, Group or Individual Performance, Group or Individual Documentary, Group or Individual Exhibit, and Group or Individual Website. You will work on this project during the year inside of school and out with your group (or by yourself). You will be able to work until the regional competition in March, and those who advance past regionals will attend the State competition in Raleigh. If anyone advances further, you will go to the National competition at the end of the school year for several days in Maryland. National History Day is a lot of work, but it's also a lot of fun! If you love history and you love competition, this club is for you!

Meetings

Meetings will be held on varying Mondays. All members will be notified of meetings. Members should be able to check the NHD Google Calendar online for meeting dates.
