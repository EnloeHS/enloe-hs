Future Scientists

Description

Enloe Future Scientists is a club for students who wish to take science further than just learning in the classroom. We hope that our members all have a passion for science and aspire to pursue it later in life. We conduct awesome experiments, discuss new discoveries, and even take part in the North Carolina Science and Engineering Fair.

Meetings

Typically afterschool on Tuesdays, but will be announced on the lodown, once or twice a month in one of Mr. Potter's rooms for an hour.
