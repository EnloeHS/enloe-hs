Science Olympiad

Description

Science Olympiad competitions consist of a series of 23 team events in each division (Division B is middle school, Division C is high school). Each year, a portion of the events are rotated to reflect the ever-changing nature of genetics, earth science, chemistry, anatomy, physics, geology, mechanical engineering and technology. By combining events from all these different fields and disciplines, Science Olympiad encourages a wide variety of students to get involved in this active, hands-on experience.
