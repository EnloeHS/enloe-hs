Serious About Math and Science (SAMS) Club

Description

SAMS club serves to promote the interests in mathematics and science of students. We combine guest lectures with hands-on demonstrations and experiments to allow the students to explore the vast worlds of math and science.

Meetings

Meetings are usually biweekly.
