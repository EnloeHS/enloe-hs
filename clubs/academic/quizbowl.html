Enloe Quiz Bowl Team

Description

The purpose of the Enloe Quiz Bowl Team shall be to harness the naturally occurring brilliance of the pupils at Enloe, enriching it and fostering it in a competitive environment. We shall bring together the intelligence that can be found at Enloe and apply it to trivia-style competition. Activities will include intensive individual and group-oriented preparation for local, state, and national competitions. Throughout the year, the Enloe Quiz Bowl Team shall compete and practice consistently, fine-tuning not only knowledge, but speed, group dynamics, and efficiency.
