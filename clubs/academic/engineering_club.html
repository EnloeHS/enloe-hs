Enloe Engineering Club

Description

Enloe Engineering Club is dedicated to informing students of the different fields and opportunities that engineering has to offer. The club organizes participation in engineering related competitions and invites guest speakers to come and talk to the club about their line of work and how they got there. Engineering Club's main focus is competing in the Technology Student Association (TSA), a national student organization devoted to teaching technology education to young people. TSA's mission is to inspire its student members to prepare for careers in a technology-driven economy and culture. Every year TSA has a state level and a national level conference for many technology related events, including Architectural Modeling, Computer-Aided Design, Electronic Game Design, Medical Technology, and more.

The club prepares a team to compete in the events at the state wide tournament that is held in March at the Four Seasons Hotel in Greensboro, NC. The top winners move onto the national level, which is held this year in Grapevine, Texas.

More information about the competition can be found here: http://tsaweb.org/High-School-Competitions. For a complete list of detailed competition events, visit the TSA Competitve Events Overview.
