Odyssey of the Mind

Description

Odyssey of the Mind is an awesome club where you can express your creative side at the competition in March. There are five long term problems which you can work with a team to solve and present at the competition as well as a spontaneous. In the spontaneous, the judges give you a problem to solve under a time limit. This tests your teams' ability to work together under pressure! Odyssey of the Mind is a fun and memorable experience!

Meetings

Meetings are usually once or twice a month on Thursdays. As a whole, we meet in Mrs. Downey's room (712). However, each team of 5-7 people will meet on their own time to work on their challenge.
