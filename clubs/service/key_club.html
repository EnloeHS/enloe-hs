Enloe Key Club

Description

Enloe Key Club is the biggest club at Enloe and the largest Key Club in North Carolina. Key Club is a service club affiliated with Kiwanis and each member is required to volunteer for 50 hours a year at community events, coordinated through the club. It is a great way to meet new people and get involved in local events!

Meetings

First Thursday of every month in the 700 Learning Lab
