Food Ark

Description

The Food Ark is an organization that aims to balance the unbalanced equation of **food insecurity** through our program's 3 branches: Garden, Food Pantry, and Outreach.

**Food insecurity** is the lack of necessary food, whether it be healthy foods or food in general. There is currently a depreciation of food in almost every city, thus we are trying to change that (hence the unbalanced equation - food to people).

There are 3 main branches of the club:

-Outreach Branch: We are partnering with local organizations such as IFFS, Community Food Lab, etc, in hosting events for Enloe students to engage in the food community.

-Garden Branch: We are currently gardening at Enloe HS (behind the 600 hall, in the courtyard, greenhouse, planter boxes around the school).

-Food Pantry Branch: We are partnering with IFFS and Enloe to bring the Food Pantry and their mission more awareness throughout our community and our school.
