Webmasters

Meetings: Tuesdays at 2:45pm in the Media Center Computer Lab.

Webmasters maintains the Enloe Website. "We control the horizontal; we control the vertical." We typed what you see now. We teach computer languages and basic programming codes. We work on updating content and graphics on the site that helps guide us through our Enloe career. If you don't know much about computers, don't be afraid to come and learn! We work on a variety of subjects that help keep the website up to date.

The new, minimalist layout of this website was designed originally by Enloe Webmasters Club of 2014-2015.
