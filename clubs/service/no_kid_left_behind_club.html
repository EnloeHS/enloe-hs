No Kid Left Behind

Service projects weekly (Wednesdays) at various elementary schools.

No Kid Left Behind is a service club which commits its time to helping out the elementary schoolers in the Raleigh area. Students provide the supplemental education needed to help the kids achieve or can act as a teacher's aide and assist with classroom activities. Meeting days TBD, but service projects held weekly (on Wednesdays).
