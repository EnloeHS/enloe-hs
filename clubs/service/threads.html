Threads for Hope

Description

Threads for Hope is a club at Enloe High School that is dedicated to improving the lives of citizens around the world by creating and donating handmade items made be members to different charities and communities.

Enloe Threads for Hope has three main goals:

  1. To use our needlework skills, whether sewing, crocheting or knitting, to create items that can be donated to various charities. These items will be distributed to those who would benefit from them most.
  2. To reach out not only to the residents in our community, but also to those across the globe. We want to make as great of an impact as possible on both a large and small scale.
  3. To spread our knowledge in sewing, crocheting, and knitting to others. These days, needlework skills are diminishing in popularity. We would like to give Enloe's students a wonderful reason to learn: the thought of giving back and using your talents for the greater good.
