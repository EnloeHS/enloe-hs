FCE (Fellowship of Christian Eagles)

Description

FCE, or Fellowship of Christian Eagles, is a Christian club that meets every Thursday in room 2609 from 2:35 to 4:00. We begin our meetings with a time of fellowship and snacks, which is followed by a Bible lesson, and finished with a game. We also have lunch Bible studies every Tuesday during each lunch period, and prayer meetings every Monday morning. All are welcome!

Meetings

Thursdays from 2:35 to 4:00 in Room 2609
