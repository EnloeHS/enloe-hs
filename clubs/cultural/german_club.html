German Club

Description

German Club is advised by the Enloe German teacher, Herr Kandah. The club participates in a variety of activities that celebrate German culture and heritage, such as making gingerbread houses in December and discussing holidays like St. Martinstag and Volkstrauertag.

Meetings

Monthly on Fridays after school in room 618.

Resources

For more information, look our the official club website at http://enloedeutschverein.blogspot.com. Prospective members can contact enloedeutschverein@gmail.com with any further questions or concerns.
