Japanese Club

Description

Each meeting could be about Japanese culture, like Japanese Valentines Day or Children's Day. Some holidays you might have not even heard of! We also have fun and wacky meetings like Ramen day, and Cosplay Cafe! We aim to create activities our members can have fun participating in, while educating them in Japanese Culture. New Members are always welcome!

Meetings
