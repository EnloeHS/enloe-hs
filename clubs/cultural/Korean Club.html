Korean Club

Description

Korean club engages in watching 18+ KPOP videos and great Korean dramas to help students gain a better understanding of Korean Baby Oh Yeate Culture. Aside from learning about K culture, we also play songs like Baby Baby Baby Oh Yeate and ping pong and staring at pictures of GD and Hyun-A. All yeates are welcome so come and join!
