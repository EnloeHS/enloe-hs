Italian Club

Description

Every month is a different cultural activity. Carnevale, Easter, Christmas, Music and dance. Let's not forget food. In the fall I get to cook them an authentic Italian Meal! We visit Italian Food Stores and enjoy Italian Gelato!

Meetings

Once a month from 2:30 until 4:00 in Room 0721.
