Our Message

Enloe High School prepares all students for the global society by giving them a broad spectrum of opportunities to prepare for education and employment beyond high school. Enloe High School is a community dedicated to a standard of excellence in education that fosters scholarship, integrity, diversity and equity.

Homebase Student Access 2015-2016

Beginning July 6, 2015, student access to Home Base/PowerSchool will be replaced by a single sign-on process offered through NCEdCloud. Students must claim their account through the NCEdCloud to access PowerSchool.

To claim your NCEdCloud Account, visit http://my.ncedcloud.org using Google Chrome or Firefox and follow the instructions to "Claim My Account".

There are no log in changes for Homebase Parent Access.

Off Campus Lunch Pass applications will be available online and in the Main Office (west Building) beginning July 1

Any Junior or Senior may apply for an off campus lunch pass. The cost for the year is $10. Passes will be processed and available for pick-up only on Friday, August 14. New applications and those received after August 13 will be processed and/or available for pick up on Friday, August 21 from 9-11 and during lunches on Monday, August 24, Tuesday, August 25, and Wednesday, August 26.
