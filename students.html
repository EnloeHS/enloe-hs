Students

Here, you can access key information for students.

Student Services

The mission of the school counselor is to provide a continuum of support services that positively impact the development of the whole child through the school, family, and community. Student Services operates with goals of developing academic success and life skills, and fostering personal and social development in students.

Career Services

Enloe's Career Development Coordinator assists students with career information, career assessments, armed services  information, job shadowing opportunities, and post-secondary transition plans. She serves as the school's liaison with local business leaders, posts information on career development opportunities, and provides current job market information for students.   Career Services offer a multitude of print and visual media resources  as well as  computer access for student use located in room 1934 in the East Building.

Drivers Ed

NC drivers education requirements, how to register for a class, driver education fees, class schedule/payment, behind the wheel policy, and obtaining your learners permit.

Testing Information

Testing information pertaining to ACT, SAT, PSAT, EOC and AP.

Library

The media-technology program prepares dynamic learners in an information rich environment to be resourceful, reflective, and responsible readers and users of information.

JROTC

JROTC is a high school program aimed at teaching students the importance of values like diversity, leadership, student achievement, and wellness.

International Baccalaureate Studies

The International Baccalaureate organization aims to develop inquiring, knowledgeable and caring young people who help to create a better and more peaceful world through intercultural understanding and respect.
